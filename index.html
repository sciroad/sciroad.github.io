About

I'm a Computer Science MSc graduate from Yıldız Technical University and a recipient of the YLSY Scholarship, Turkey's most prestigious award for doctoral study abroad. I'm currently applying to PhD programs in the US.

My research philosophy centers on a simple premise: intelligent coordination among specialized components outperforms monolithic scale. I believe effective AI systems should emerge from modular, reusable parts.
